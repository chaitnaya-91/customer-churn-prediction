Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to run customer churn predictions. This includes data you upload (such as customer usage, tenure, charges, and payment methods) and basic account credentials (name, email address).

All uploaded dataset files are processed locally on our secure servers and are not distributed, shared, or sold to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information you input or upload is used exclusively to:

  • Generate churn risk assessments and factor breakdowns.
  • Train, evaluate, and tune prediction models specifically for your accounts.
  • Provide dashboard statistics and custom history retention.
  • Improve our service algorithms and performance.

3. Data Security and Retention

We implement robust security measures to protect your user data and customer files. All database communication is encrypted using secure protocols, and sensitive passwords are encrypted via industry-standard hashing algorithms (bcrypt).

You retain complete control of your data. You may delete any uploaded datasets, trained model parameters, or prediction history from your settings dashboard at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with external third-party advertisers or data brokers. All data analysis, storage, and processing reside in our secure environment.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our data protection team directly at analytics@churnpredict.ai.
