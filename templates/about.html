Our Story

Churn Predictor was born from a simple observation: acquiring new customers costs 5-25 times more than retaining existing ones. Yet, many businesses struggle to identify at-risk customers before it's too late.

Our team of data scientists and industry experts came together to create a solution that makes advanced churn prediction accessible to businesses of all sizes. By combining cutting-edge machine learning with deep industry knowledge, we've built a platform that delivers both accuracy and actionable insights.

Today, our system helps businesses across six major industries predict and prevent customer churn, saving millions in potential revenue loss and building stronger customer relationships.
