Getting Started

1

Create an Account

Sign up for free by clicking the "Register" button. Enter your first name, last name, email, and password. After registration, you'll be automatically logged in.

2

Choose Your Industry

Navigate to the "Predict" page and select your industry from the six available options: Telecom, Banking, E-commerce, Subscription Services, Fitness Gym, or Ride Sharing.

3

Enter Customer Data

Fill in the customer information form with relevant details like tenure, charges, contract type, and payment method. The more accurate your data, the better the prediction.

4

Get Prediction Results

Receive instant churn risk assessment (High, Medium, or Low) with a detailed breakdown of risk factors and actionable recommendations to prevent customer loss.

How Our Predictions Work

Our churn prediction system uses advanced rule-based algorithms that analyze multiple customer attributes to calculate churn risk. Each factor is weighted based on its correlation with customer attrition in real-world industry data.

Risk Factors Analyzed

  • • Customer Tenure: Length of relationship with your business
  • • Monthly Charges: Regular payment amount
  • • Total Charges: Cumulative spending over time
  • • Contract Type: Commitment level (month-to-month, annual, etc.)
  • • Payment Method: How customers pay for services

Understanding Results

  • • High Risk (>60%): Immediate action required to prevent churn
  • • Medium Risk (30-60%): Monitor closely and engage proactively
  • • Low Risk (<30%): Customer appears stable and satisfied
  • • Risk Score: Percentage indicating overall churn probability
  • • Recommendations: Specific actions to reduce churn risk

Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption to protect your data. Your customer information is never shared with third parties and is used solely for generating predictions.

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

What if I need help with a specific prediction?

Our support team is here to help! Contact us through the support portal or email us directly. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Currently, we support individual customer predictions. For enterprise bulk prediction needs, please contact our sales team for custom solutions.

Which industry model should I use?

Choose the model that best matches your business type. Each model is trained on industry-specific data and optimized for that sector's unique churn patterns. If your business spans multiple industries, start with your primary revenue source.
